who we are

Mid-Cities Arts Collective, aka M.A.C., is a new nonprofit arts organization, with a focus on theater arts. This new and amazing community was built from the ashes of The Artisan Center Theater, which closed its doors over the summer. M.A.C. is community, M.A.C. is joy and fun, M.A.C. is excellence, M.A.C. is inclusive and accessible, & M.A.C. is family. Follow our IG page here and our FB page and come along for the ride! We can’t do this without you!
